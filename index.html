About

Hello! I'm a MS/BS/BA student at Stanford University majoring in computer science and minoring in music. I'm a pianist, violinist, and organist—check out my performances here!

My expertise is in NLP and AI for healthcare. I led the Stanford NLP's Team Chirpy Cardinal to the $250K 1st Place (Science Prize) in the Amazon Alexa Socialbot Grand Challenge 5. Previously, I was at was at Hudson River Trading and NVIDIA AI, served as the president of Stanford ACM and Stanford Symphony, and co-founded the Stanford Poker Tournament.

In my free time, I enjoy playing board games, solving crosswords, and writing fun linguistics puzzles. In addition to NACLO, I've contributed to MIT Mystery Hunt, and co-chaired Stanford Puzzle Hunt.
